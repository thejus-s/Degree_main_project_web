About Us

Welcome to eVoting

Our project aims to modernize college elections by introducing a user-friendly online voting platform. Developed with Python for the front end and Microsoft SQL Server for the back end, our system operates seamlessly on Windows 10 and is accessible via Google Chrome. With the integration of blockchain technology, we ensure the highest level of security and transparency, empowering students to participate confidently in the democratic process.
